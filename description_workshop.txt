[h1] Spiritcaller Ronin Mod [/h1]

[h2]Begin your journey as a mystical warrior![/h2]

Start a new journey as a mystic samurai or a powerful spiritcaller. In this Japanese-themed mod you can use magic spiritcaller weapons with different nature themed attacks or different katanas with a variety of slashes or attacks.

[h3]Features:[/h3]
[list]
[*] New NPC: Meet Sekiro from the fromsoftware game, a mysterious shinobi merchant who sells powerful eastern weapons
[*] Unique Katanas: Each with distinct combat styles and special attacks
[*] Spiritcaller Magic: Harness the power of nature with themed magical weapons
[*] Eastern Combat System: Experience fluid combat with various slash combinations
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[i]Heavily inspired by Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice and traditional samurai culture.[/i]
